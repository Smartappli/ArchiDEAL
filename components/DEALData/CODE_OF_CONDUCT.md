# Code Of Conduct

DEALData uses a pragmatic professional conduct policy.

## Expected Behavior

- Be precise, respectful, and evidence-driven.
- Critique technical decisions, data contracts, and operational choices, not people.
- Surface risk early when a change affects security, privacy, data quality, operations, or compliance.
- Respect confidentiality for datasets, pilots, adopter references, incidents, and partner discussions.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private data, credentials, endpoint values, or adopter information without approval.
- Misrepresenting production readiness, compliance status, data lineage, or adoption references.
- Intentionally bypassing security controls, review requirements, or data-protection boundaries.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content that violates this policy. Repeated or severe violations can lead to blocked participation in issues, pull requests, or community channels.

Report conduct concerns through the security or support channels in `SUPPORT.md`. Do not disclose sensitive details in public issues.
